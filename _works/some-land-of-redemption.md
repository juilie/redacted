  *Some Land of Redemption* is not a home, it is not a haunting, it is a
  culmination of dreams unfulfilled. Echos of the self hum with pleasure,
  loneliness, and dependency within the doll house. They are singing an origin
  story at the end of the world.

  **Performed by:** Sage Horsey, Cayleen Del Rosario, Demetris Charalambous, Cole Stapleton, Mimi Doan


  **Music by**: Slic


  **Costumes by:** Joohee Park


  **Run Time**: 45 minutes
